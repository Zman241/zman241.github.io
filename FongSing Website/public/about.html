About Us

We are a small, family-owned Chinese/Vietnamese restaurant that has been operating since 1996!

Here is where flavour begins. We started our restaurant journey back in 1996 and have been proudly family-owned ever since.

Come check out the diverse menu, friendly staff, and most importantly - tasty food made fresh to order!

We’re famous for our wide range of combination plates, pho, salad rolls, barbeque duck, noodle bowls, and more!

Did you know? - a bowl of pho is only ever as good as the broth, which is why we’re so proud to make our own from scratch here at Fong Sing! We also take great pride in making our hot sauce in-house as well!

Please note: table service availability is subject to change with the ongoing pandemic, refer to our Facebook page for any important updates

Prices are subject to change.

Dine with us!

Available for dine-in and takeout, come check out our cozy little spot and you'll see why the Halifax area has loved us since 1996.

Last call for dine-in service and delivery is half an hour to close (currently 7:30pm).

Delivery Policy

There is a $6.00 delivery fee for orders placed and delivered before 5pm. After 5pm, delivery is free to those paying cash at an address within 5km of the restaurant. However, if the machine is needed for payment with a card, there is a $2.00 fee. Deliveries outside of our standard 5km zone start at $5, and are based on how far away the location is. Some restrictions may apply, please call the restaurant for any further questions or details!
